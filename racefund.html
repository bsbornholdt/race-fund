Business Plan

Executive Summary

RaceFund is a competitive, community-driven platform that provides a new pathway into motorsport by crowdfunding sponsorships through simulator racing tournaments. By allowing users to pay entry fees for tournament participation, we create a direct funding model that supports the top racers’ transition into real-world racing.

Problem Statement

Professional motorsport is financially inaccessible for most aspiring drivers due to high entry costs and a lack of affordable pathways. RaceFund democratizes access to motorsport by using a crowdfunding model to raise entry fees, directly funding winners’ real-life racing opportunities.

Solution

RaceFund enables aspiring racers to leverage their online skills, while fans participate in their journey, fostering a unique community-centric sponsorship model.

Market Analysis

  • Industry Size: Esports and sim racing markets are growing rapidly, offering a large user base.
  • Target Audience: Sim racers, motorsport fans, and sponsors interested in community-driven engagement.
  • Competitive Edge: Our crowdfunding model enables accessible sponsorships, setting us apart from competitors.

Business Model

  • Revenue Streams: Entry fees, sponsorships, merchandise sales, and premium content subscriptions.
  • Cost Structure: Platform development, marketing, and operational expenses.

Product

  • Core Features: Multi-tiered tournaments, community engagement, and leaderboard tracking.
  • Future Enhancements: Real-time streaming, regional divisions, and customizable fan engagement options.

Go-to-Market Strategy

Initial launch targets sim racing communities, followed by growth through regional tournaments and brand partnerships.

Team

Our team includes seasoned professionals in esports, gaming software, community engagement, and business development.

Funding Requirements
